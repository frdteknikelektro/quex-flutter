# Quex — Video Script V4
**Format:** Product demo with context · Indonesian dialogue · English subtitles · Screen recording for all app shots

**Camera:** iPhone 17 · Tripod · Wife operating
**Editing:** CapCut
**Location:** Kids' bedroom — desk, chair, bookshelf with trophies, single bed

---

- **DIRECTOR'S NOTE**
- V4 philosophy: demo first, story as frame. Judges know what Quex does by 0:17. Every remaining second is proof.

- Airplane mode is ON before the demo starts. It stays visible in the status bar throughout. No toggle scene mid-demo.
- Estimated cut duration: ~1:48–2:00. Target: under 3:00. On the short side — judges will not be bored.
