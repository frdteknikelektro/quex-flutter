# Quex — Video Script V2
**Format:** Real footage · Spoken Indonesian · English subtitles
**Target length:** 3:00–3:15
**Camera:** iPhone 17 · Tripod · Wife operating
**Location:** Kids' bedroom (desk, chair, bookshelf with trophies, single bed)
**Cast:** Farid (Dad), Kindi (8), Raina (9)

---

> **DIRECTOR'S NOTE — SHOW, DON'T TELL**
> No narration. No title cards explaining things. Every emotion is read from faces.
> The room is small — use it. Tight frames. Real mess. Real ceiling light. Don't clean the room.
> iPhone 17 Cinematic Mode ON for all close-ups. 4K 30fps for everything else.
> Shoot at night. The single ceiling bulb creates exactly the right mood.

## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Shot Order for Recording Day
Shoot in this order to minimize setup changes:

| Order | Shots | Setup |
|-------|-------|-------|
| 1st | 1, 2, 9, 10 | Dad at desk — close-ups and medium |
| 2nd | 3 | Laptop screen Google Calendar (shoot separately, easy to redo) |
| 3rd | 4, 6, 14 | Wide shots from door and corner |
| 4th | 5, 7, 8 | Kids at door — low angle |
| 5th | 11, 12, 13, 15 | Kids struggling on bed |
| 6th | 16, 17, 18, 19 | Handoff — dad on bed |
| 7th | 20–30 | App demo — OTS and iPad ECU |
| 8th | 31–35 | Score + hug + breath beat |
| 9th | 36–40 | Friend conversation — most takes here |
| 10th | 41, 42 | Next day entry — open curtain for daylight |
| 11th | 43, 44 | Exam paper close-up + dad's face |
| Last | 45 | Final wide room shot |

### iPhone 17 Settings
- **Cinematic Mode ON:** Shots 2, 5, 7, 8, 9, 15, 19, 24, 26, 29, 33, 37, 38, 39, 40, 44
- **4K 30fps regular:** Wide shots, iPad screen ECU, action shots
- **Exposure lock:** Tap and hold on face before recording each close-up — ceiling light will fool auto-exposure
- **Do NOT use flash or torch** — the warm single ceiling bulb IS the mood

### Key Performances
1. **Shot 9** — dad's pause after "Mama kerja malam" — 3 full seconds of silence before he speaks. Don't cut early.
2. **Shot 27** — dad watching kids learn, side-frame — he should not perform. Just watch. Real curiosity.
3. **Shot 38/39 intercut** — Raina's friend line. Multiple takes. Use the flattest, most natural delivery.
4. **Shot 40** — "Boleh." — one word, then 4 seconds silence. Don't cut early.
5. **Shot 45** — final wide shot — nobody should move or "perform" for camera. Just exist in the room.

### Audio
- Record with phone microphone — small room at close range is fine
- TTS from app (Shot 25) — unmute device, let it play out loud naturally
- No music anywhere until Shot 45, second 3
- Background music: soft, neutral, instrumental. Nothing that competes with the story.
- In post: add light ambient room tone under night scenes (fan hum, distant sounds) if the room feels too dead

### What NOT to do
- Don't clean the room. The clutter — bags, clothes, books on floor — is authentic and makes the room feel lived-in.
- Don't add ring lights. The ceiling bulb warmth is the look.
- Don't over-direct the kids. Tell them what happens, not how to feel. Their natural imperfect delivery is more real than polished acting.
- Don't show the Quex app name as a title card early. Let it appear on screen organically.
- Don't add music before Shot 45. The silence is doing work the whole film.
