# Quex — Video Script
**Target length: ~2.5 minutes**

---

## SCENE 1 — Hook (0:00–0:20)

**[Screen: black / title card]**

> *Narration:*
> "My wife and I both work. Sometimes she's on a night shift. Sometimes I'm racing a deadline.
> And sometimes... our kids have an exam tomorrow."

**[Screen: fade in to phone home screen — no WiFi icon, low signal]**

> "We wanted to help. But we were tired. And honestly — we didn't always understand the material ourselves."

**[Screen: Quex app icon, tap to open]**

> "So I built what I wish existed."

---

## SCENE 2 — Product intro (0:20–0:35)

**[Screen: splash screen — Quex logo + duck mascot, model downloading]**

> *Narration:*
> "Quex. A patient AI tutor that lives on the phone.
> Knows exactly what your kid is studying.
> No WiFi required. No monthly fees. No data sent anywhere."

**[Screen: download completes → profile selection grid]**

> "Each kid has their own profile."

## SCENE 3 — Core demo (0:35–1:55)

**[Screen: profile tap → home screen with study sessions]**

> *Narration:*
> "Let's say your daughter has a Science exam tomorrow."

**[Screen: create new session — type "Science Chapter 5"]**

**[Screen: tap "Add Material" → camera opens → photograph a textbook page]**

> "She photographs her textbook page."

**[Screen: photo uploads, "Generate Quiz" button appears]**

> "Quex reads it — on device — using Gemma 4."

**[Screen: quiz generating animation → first question appears]**

> "And builds a quiz from that exact material."

**[Screen: kid answers a question incorrectly — wrong answer highlighted]**

> "She gets one wrong."

**[Screen: tap "Help me understand" → chat opens]**

> "She taps 'Help me.'"

**[Screen: AI tutor response appears, TTS reads it aloud]**

> *Narration (quieter, over TTS audio):*
> "The AI explains it. As many times as she needs.
> No judgment. No rushing."

**[Screen: she tries the question again — correct answer]**

## Recording Notes

| Scene | What to capture |
|-------|----------------|
| Splash / download | Show model already downloaded (fast) OR show download progress bar briefly |
| Profile selection | Netflix-style grid — looks great on camera |
| Material upload | Use a real printed textbook page. Photograph it naturally. |
| Quiz generation | Let the animation play — shows AI "thinking" |
| Wrong answer | Pick a question you know the answer to so the wrong-then-right arc is clean |
| Help me / chat | Let TTS play out loud — unmute device audio for recording |
| No WiFi moment | Turn on airplane mode before this scene for authenticity |

**Tone:** Personal, quiet, honest. Not a product pitch — a story.
**Music:** Soft, neutral background. Nothing that competes with TTS audio.
**Captions:** Add for accessibility (required for non-English-speaking judges too).
